About Me

Darius' Freshman Class

I'm Darius Daughtry, a 16-year-old music connoisseur. Hip hop is my favorite genre of music and I'm a big fan of the XXL Freshman Class that gets published annually. I love the idea of giving lesser known artists a platform for exposure to new fans. My Freshman class is very similar, almost identical in concept to XXL's, but there are a few key differences.

For starters, I'm the only one who has influence in who makes the list, hence MY name being in the title. That being said if YOUR favorite artist isn't on the list, you can't say I'm wrong because it is MY opinion. Second off, my list is much more exclusive than XXL. They usually include 10-12 artists in their Freshman Class. I only chose 4. Lastly, The level of popularity of these artists is lower than that of artists XXL would pick. None of these artists have gold or platinum plaques and none of them have more than 1.5 million spotify monthly listeners as of July 29, 2021.
